Brigadeiro

Brigadeiro is a beloved Brazilian sweet, known for its creamy texture and deep chocolate flavor. Itâ€™s simple, quick, and perfect for celebrations or satisfying a sweet craving.

Traditionally rolled into bite-sized balls and covered in chocolate sprinkles, brigadeiro is a nostalgic treat for many Brazilians.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (395g) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • Chocolate sprinkles (for coating)

Steps

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, add the condensed milk, butter, and cocoa powder.
  2. Stir continuously to avoid burning.
  3. Cook until the mixture thickens and pulls away from the bottom of the pan (about 8 to 10 minutes).
  4. Transfer to a plate and let it cool.
  5. Grease your hands with a little butter, roll the mixture into small balls.
  6. Coat each ball with chocolate sprinkles.
  7. Store in a cool place or refrigerate until firm.
