Pancakes

Pancakes are a classic breakfast favoriteâ€”soft, fluffy, and incredibly easy to prepare. They pair perfectly with honey, butter, fruits, or your favorite syrup.

This simple recipe creates light, golden pancakes that cook quickly and make for a comforting start to the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add the milk, egg, and melted butter; whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour a small amount of batter onto the pan.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side.
  6. Repeat until all batter is used.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
